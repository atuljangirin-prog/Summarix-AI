    Frequently asked questions

    Everything you might want to know.

    How does the summarization work?

    We score each sentence based on word frequency (excluding stopwords), position in the document, and length, then pick the top-ranked sentences in their original order.

    What PDFs are supported?

    Any text-based PDF. Scanned image-only PDFs need OCR, which isn't included by default.

    Is my document private?

    Yes. Everything runs in your browser — no file ever leaves your device.

    Can I download the summary?

    Yes, copy to clipboard or download as a .txt file.

    Does it use a real AI model?

    It uses lightweight NLP-style algorithms tuned for speed and clarity, all client-side.
